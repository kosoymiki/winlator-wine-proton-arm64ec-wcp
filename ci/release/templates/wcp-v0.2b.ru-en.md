## EN
- This release bundles `wine-11-arm64ec`, `proton-ge10-arm64ec`, and `protonwine10-gamenative-arm64ec`.
- These are **content packages** for Winlator CMOD Aero.so and are downloaded via `Contents`.
- Channels are split into `stable` (tag `wcp-v0.2b`) and per-package rolling nightly tags (`wine-11-arm64ec-latest`, `proton-ge10-arm64ec-latest`, `protonwine10-gamenative-arm64ec-latest`).
- Every `.wcp` includes a forensic manifest (`share/wcp-forensics/*`).
